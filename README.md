# YouTube-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI

· Analysed 11.47bn total views across 537 titles tracking 6 KPIs — total views, engagement rate, views per day, like ratio, comment ratio & viral score
· Identified top-performing channels & titles by daily views, revealing short-duration content drives higher engagement — supporting content strategy decisions
· Uncovered 78% of titles lack captions & content upload volume surged post-2022 — exposing accessibility gaps & rapid platform growth trends
· Mapped duration vs. engagement rate across all titles, revealing shorter videos consistently outperform longer content in audience retention
